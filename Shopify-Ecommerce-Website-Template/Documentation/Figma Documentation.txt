Instructions for unpacking product:
==================================

To modify this file,  The Figma software must be installed on your device.
You can download Figma from https://www.figma.com/downloads/
=================

Extract the contents of the .zip file into a folder of your choice.

Open the extracted folder.

Double-click on "Planti.fig" to open the file in Figma.

Modify the file as desired and save your changes."
